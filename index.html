FortressOne

Quake Team Fortress Remastered

FortressOne is a free, open source fork of the original 1996 QuakeWorld mod Team Fortress. It is a fast-paced, class based, arena first person shooter remastered with refined graphics, new mechanics and evolved gameplay.

Join us on Discord, fork us on GitHub or watch us play on YouTube and Twitch.

Download

Latest release: 9th August 2021

FortressOne is a stand-alone game. Download the zip file, extract and run.
